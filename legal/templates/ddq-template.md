> **Note for systematic managers:** Many institutional LPs have ESG reporting requirements even for funds that are not ESG-focused. Be prepared to explain why your strategy does or does not incorporate ESG factors and what the rationale is. A defensible "we are agnostic to ESG because our signals are price-based and factor-based, not fundamental" answer is acceptable to most LPs if stated clearly and consistently.
