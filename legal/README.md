# Legal — Document Index

> **Disclaimer:** These templates are starting points for attorney review. They do not constitute legal advice. Engage qualified fund counsel before executing any legal documents or making any regulatory filings. Fund formation involves securities law, tax law, and commodity regulation — errors have material consequences. The cost of competent counsel at formation is a fraction of the cost of fixing mistakes after launch.

## Sequencing Guidance

For a first-time systematic fund manager, the recommended order is:

1. **Read the Delaware LLC Checklist** — form the management company first. Nothing else can happen without a legal entity.
2. **Read the NFA CPO Exemption Guide** — determine whether you qualify for the 4.13(a)(3) exemption or need to register as a CPO.
3. **Read the IA Registration Guide** — determine your investment adviser registration obligations at launch and as you scale.
4. **Engage fund counsel** — bring the Operating Agreement Outline and LP Subscription Agreement Outline to your first attorney meeting. This compresses the time you spend in billable intake.
5. **Prepare the DDQ** — complete a draft DDQ before your first LP meeting. Sophisticated LPs will ask for it; having it ready signals operational readiness.
